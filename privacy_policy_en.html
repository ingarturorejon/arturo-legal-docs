This Privacy Policy describes how Arturo González Rejón ("we", "our", or "the developer") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile applications available on Google Play Store.

By downloading, installing, or using our applications, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Our applications may automatically collect certain technical information, including:

  • Device type and model

1.2 Information We DO NOT Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as:

The collected information is used to:

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to show personalized ads. You can review Google's privacy policy at: https://policies.google.com/privacy
